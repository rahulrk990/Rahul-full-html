Compucom Institute Of Information Technology And Management, Jaipur is affiliated with RTU and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). It offers B.Tech, BCA at the UG level, and MBA, MCA, and M. Tech at the PG level. For admission in B.Tech candidates must have valid scores in REAP, and for MBA valid scores in RMAP. The institute provides 100% placement assistance to its students. The highest Package was offered at INR 14.7 LPA and the average package was at INR 3.6LPA, Industry exposure, and Internship facilities.
